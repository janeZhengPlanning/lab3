The Latest about Jane...

Jane Zheng published an article titled “Structuring Artistic Creativity for The Production of a ‘Creative City’: Urban Sculpture Planning in Shanghai” in the recent issue (issue 45, no.5, 2021) of the International Journal of Urban and Regional Research.

A four-year research project titled “Formation and Transformation of the Modern Urban Art Scene: A Historical GIS Approach to the “Creative Milieu” of Republican Shanghai, 1912–1948,” funded by the Hong Kong University Research Council, was completed.
